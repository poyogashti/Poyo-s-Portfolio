Who Am I?

For the most part, I'm still figuring that one out. The details, however, I have assumptions about; I love visual arts. There is a certain quality that allows me to communicate a feeling with something other than words—the pure expression of moments.
Photography has been an excellent way for me to study expressions. The simple way everything comes together so proper to the moment at heart yet so perplexing to the mind makes it a perfect language.

Sea Choir

When did you dance last? This image is the first time I saw a school of fish. Moving, dancing, flowing across the water like an orchestra on ice.
Flowing with freedom and connection, nothing could stop them. I am so inspired by this glimpse of a connection so grand that paints a canvas such as this.

This is just a beginning

As the wheels of fortune turn, my life comes to a new chapter. I am embarking on yet another odyssey, but with way more at stake this time. After losing my home, I have decided to take the road and see the world with a new eye and experience meanings with a different intensity. I am starting with nomads and documenting a cultural life that is bewitching even to my countrymen. I see this as an opportunity to dive into the lives of humans even further.
